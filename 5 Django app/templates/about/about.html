Welcome to English Football League Analysis

A comprehensive analytical platform for exploring trends and patterns in English League Football from the first season (1888-1889) to the most recently created season (2024-2025). Our application provides detailed insights into trends over time and the factors that influence goals scored and conceded. We offer this analysis through interactive visualizations.

Features

  • Trends Analysis: Explore historical data and identify patterns in the data.
  • Goals Analysis: Deep dive into the factors that influence goals scored and conceded.
  • Club Analysis: Explore detailed club history, league progression, and match data.
  • Interactive Charts: Powered by Bokeh, our visualizations provide rich, interactive data exploration.
  • User Management: Secure authentication and role-based access control.
